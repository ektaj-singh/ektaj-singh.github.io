My strengths are that I’m a very fast learning, I pick up on things quickly and especially if I get to see how its done as I’m a very visual leaner, my other strengths also include me being a attentive listener, someone who is open to ideas and gives feedback. My limitations are my lack of recapping and going over material multiple times this is something I struggle with and hope to improve on through my time at Dev.
